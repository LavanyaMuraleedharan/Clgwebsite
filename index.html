About Our College

BIT-Durg, established in 1986, is committed to academic excellence and innovation in education, offering graduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines. The institute has grown tremendously over the years and now offers several graduate and postgraduate courses. It is known for its commitment to academic excellence and innovation in education.

Principal's Message

Bhilai Institute Of Technology, Durg, with a mission to provide world-class technical manpower to serve the industry, profession, and society, and to contribute effectively to the national economic development, has acquired the status of one of the premier technological institutions of India.Our dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and a strong focus on academic and professional excellence ensure that our students are well-prepared to make a positive impact in the world.

Achievements

  • Bhilai Institute of Technology, Durg is the first Technical Institute to be conferred with the Autonomous status w.e.f. 2020-2021.
  • Institute is also Accredited by NAAC – A grade cycle 1 w.e.f Sept 2017.
  • Institute has been NIRF – 2020 ranked under Engineering Category in the rank band of 251-300.
